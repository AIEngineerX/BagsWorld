    About

    BagsDungeon is a 2D MMORPG dungeon adventure inside BagsWorld. Explore, fight mobs, defeat bosses, and collect loot — all from within the BagsWorld ecosystem on Solana. Powered by the open-source Kaetram engine (MPL-2.0). Original game engine by Veradictus / OmniaDev, based on BrowserQuest by Mozilla / Little Workshop.
