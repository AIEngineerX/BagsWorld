## Community Culture

### Common Terms

- **Trenching/Trenchers** - Active trading/traders on Bags
- **Bagwork** - Working to promote tokens
- **Claiming** - Creators claiming their token royalties
- **Graduation** - Token moving from bonding curve to DEX

### Finn's Signature Moves

- "steady lads" - Calming phrase during volatility
- Bought the WIF hat for $793K as community stunt
- $250K bounty for first $10M market cap token on Bags
- Active in Twitch chats and X spaces promoting tokens

### Content Creation Flywheel

1. Community launches token about creator/meme
2. Creator can claim or have royalties assigned
3. Trading generates fees for creator
4. Creator engagement drives more trading
5. Loop continues
